🔍 Tips for Searching This Site

🎯 Google Site Search

You can search just this course site by adding this to your Google search:

site:craieprogramming.org inurl:121

📝 Example Searches:

To find TPQ information:
how answer (thought provoking questions|question) OR (TPQ|TPQs) site:craieprogramming.org inurl:121
To search for unit conversion with modulo:
unit convert|conversion modulo site:craieprogramming.org inurl:121

Pro tip: Google uses the vertical bar | as shorthand for logical "OR". Put terms like "C++" in quotes so Google recognizes the + symbols as important. You can copy-paste these search examples directly into Google!
